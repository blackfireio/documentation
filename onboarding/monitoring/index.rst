:doc:`Blackfire monitoring </monitoring-cookbooks/index>` provides a real-time
bird's-eye view of your application performance.

Designed to swiftly bootstrap your performance improvement investigations,
Blackfire monitoring informs you on **when and where** activity happened,
allowing you to start drilling down the issue.

Once identified, :doc:`Blackfire Profiler </profiling-cookbooks/index>`
helps you spot the root cause of the function or service call level.

Blackfire monitoring also lets you prioritize possible improvements by always
displaying the impact of :term:`transactions <Transaction>`
and :term:`spans <Span>`.

Alongside :doc:`alerting </monitoring-cookbooks/alerting>` and
:doc:`health reports </monitoring-cookbooks/health-report>`,
Blackfire monitoring is designed to help you save time and regain control
over your application's performance.
